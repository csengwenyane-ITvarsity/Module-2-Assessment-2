Our Story

Founded in 1995, Pine City Mall has been the heart of Pine City's retail and entertainment scene for nearly three decades. What started as a modest shopping center has grown into a vibrant community hub that serves over 2 million visitors annually.

Our commitment to providing an exceptional shopping experience, diverse retail options, and world-class entertainment has made us the preferred destination for families, professionals, and tourists alike.
